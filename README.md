# 🌍 EcoGuardian AI

EcoGuardian AI is an AI-powered Environmental Pollution Detection and Guardian System that monitors real-time air quality and weather conditions, analyzes environmental risks, and provides intelligent recommendations to protect public health.

Unlike traditional monitoring systems that only display pollution data, EcoGuardian AI acts as an **Environmental First-Aid Assistant**, helping users understand pollution levels and guiding them on the actions they should take during critical environmental conditions.

The platform integrates Air Quality Index (AQI) monitoring, weather analysis, AI-powered environmental assistance, data analytics, and automated report generation to promote environmental awareness and sustainable decision-making.

**Tagline:** *Monitoring Pollution, Protecting Lives Through AI.*
